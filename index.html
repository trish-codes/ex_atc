Atc Int’l is a business solutions company based in Houston, TX. We have global presence, reaching as far as Africa’s beautiful island country Equatorial Guinea and India, to the Coasts of the great U. S. A. We offer business management consulting, brand management, design and multimedia solutions to businesses of all sizes. Our portfolio also includes expanded business solutions offered by our international network of business partners. Beyond our business solutions, we share our world of #allthingscreative thru a variety of art forms, including music, art and videos and other mediums of creative expression, celebrating #allthingscreative.

At Atc, Int’l, we aim to creatively impact the way the world experiences our clients’ businesses, our company and #allthingscreative!

At Atc Int’l we are thinkers, doers and storytellers who inspire through art, design, media, words, code and creativity. we don’t use the “B” word except when asking “What Box?” our unlimited potential and desire to create is unmatched, only to be proceeded by our desire to inspire through creative solutions!
